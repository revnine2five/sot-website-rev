Our Background
SneakersorTrainers

SneakersOrTrainers is a young and dynamic online retailer of athletic footwear that was established in Denpasar City in 2020 by Revandra Sucirta and Arin Laksmi. The founders shared a passion for sneakers and recognized the growing demand for high-quality athletic footwear.

Our Integrity to All of You

"Our Integrity to All of You" is a business program developed by SneakersOrTrainers that emphasizes the importance of integrity in all aspects of its operations. The program focuses on providing customers with high-quality products and services while maintaining honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior.

SneakersOrTrainers believes that integrity is critical to building strong relationships with its customers and partners, and as such, it is committed to upholding these values throughout all facets of its business. From sourcing materials and manufacturing processes to customer service and marketing, the program ensures that integrity is at the forefront of every decision made. With "Our Integrity to all of you," SneakersOrTrainers aims to set a new standard for ethical and transparent business practices in the industry.
